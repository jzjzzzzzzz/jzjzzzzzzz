Research

PUBLISHED

Hummingbird-Inspired Reinforcement-Learning-Driven Flapping-Wing Robot

Developed a biomimetic flapping-wing robot for stable low-speed flight and water-air cross-medium operation. Designed lightweight mechanical platform with figure-eight wing motion, crank-rocker transmission, carbon-fiber frame, and RL-based stabilization control.

Published in the International Conference on Mathematical Physics and Computational Simulation (ISSN: 2753-8818), indexed by CPCI and Google Scholar. Received Honorable Mention at Mercer Science Fair.

Sep 2025 – Mar 2026
DEPLOYED

New Jersey Nitrate Hotspot & Water Quality Dashboard

Analyzed 2000–2026 WQP nitrate data, cleaning 5.2M+ records and extracting 56,007 nitrate observations. Identified 411 hotspot stations across 3,658 monitoring sites using mean and p90 thresholds.

Built and deployed a public Flask dashboard linking nitrate hotspots with watersheds, land use, and NJPDES facility density.
